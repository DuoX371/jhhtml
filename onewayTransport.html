What is One Way Car?

One Way Car is a car-booking app that revolutionizes the way you move by sending a private vehicle along your way at the tap of a button! A brainchild of One Way, Southeast Asia’s leading transportation technology app, One Way Car is your new favorite ride in the metro. We take you where you want to go in chauffeur-style service. With One Way Car, you don’t just book a ride– you book an experience.

Why Should You Use One Way Car?

Enjoy the certainty of fixed fares, insurance coverage and quality drivers with every ride.

Transparent, Fixed Fares

Know your exact fare even before you book.


Personal Accident Insurance

We’ve got your back from the moment you enter a One Way Car.


Quality Drivers

Every One Way Car driver is rigorously screened and rated (by you) to ensure you get the best service.

What is One Way Share?

One Way Share is a shared One Way ride that allows you to spilt the cost of your ride with a stranger who is headed in a similiar direction as you.

Why One Way Share?

Share & Save

Share rides with anyone on your way to your destination


Lower Fare Rates up to 30%

Spend up to 30% less on fares when you book a One Way Share.


Save the Environment

Carpooling reduces your carbon footprint.
